# CampusCoin Mobile Application  
## Complete User Guide and Usage Instructions

This document provides a complete step-by-step guide to creating an account, securing your wallet, and using all major features of the CampusCoin mobile application.

---

# 1. Account Registration

When opening the application for the first time, you must create an account.

![Create Account Screen](docs/images/signup.png)

### Steps to Register

1. Tap **Create Account**.
2. Enter your institutional email address.

   Example:
   - B23CN082@kitsw.ac.in  
   - b23cn082@kitsw.ac.in  

3. Enter your **Student ID**.

   Example:
   - B23CN081  

4. Create a password.

### Password Requirements

- Minimum 8 characters  
- At least one uppercase letter  
- At least one lowercase letter  
- At least one number  
- At least one special character  

5. Tap **Create Account**.

If the details are valid, your account will be created successfully.

---

# 2. Setting Your 6-Digit Security PIN

After registration, you must create a 6-digit PIN to secure your wallet.

![Create PIN Screen](docs/images/pin.png)

### Steps

1. Enter any 6-digit numeric PIN.
2. Confirm the PIN (if prompted).

### Important Security Rule

Every time you close the application and reopen it, you must enter your 6-digit PIN to access your wallet.

---

# 3. Home Dashboard Overview

After logging in with your PIN, you will see the main dashboard.

![Home Screen](docs/images/home.png)

The dashboard displays:

- Your current CampusCoin balance
- Quick action buttons:
  - Send
  - My QR
  - History
- Recent transaction activity

---

# 4. Sending CampusCoin Tokens

To transfer tokens to another user:

![Send Tokens Screen](docs/images/send.png)

### Steps to Send Tokens

1. Tap **Send**.
2. Enter the recipient's Student ID  
   (The same Student ID they used during account creation)

   OR

   Tap the QR icon and scan the recipient’s QR code.

3. Enter the amount of CampusCoin (CC) to send.
4. Tap **Send**.

### Transaction Outcome

The transfer will succeed if:

- The recipient account exists.
- You have sufficient balance.

The application will show an error if:

- The recipient does not exist.
- Your balance is insufficient.
- Backend connectivity is unavailable.

---

# 5. Viewing Your QR Code

Tap **My QR** to display your personal QR code.

Other users can scan your QR code to send tokens directly to your account.

---

# 6. Transaction History

To view all transactions:

![Transaction History Screen](docs/images/history.png)

Tap **History** to see:

- Sent transactions
- Received transactions
- Minted tokens (if applicable)
- Transaction status (Success or Failed)
- Date and time of each transaction

All transaction records are stored on the blockchain ledger.

---

# 7. Profile Section

![Profile Screen](docs/images/profile.png)

In the Profile section, you can view:

- Student ID
- Email address
- Role
- Account status

You can also log out from this section.

---

# 8. Security and Access Behavior

- The 6-digit PIN is required every time the app is reopened.
- Do not share your password or PIN.
- All transactions require backend and blockchain connectivity.
- If the network is unavailable, transactions will not be processed.

---

# Conclusion

CampusCoin provides a secure and blockchain-backed campus token system.  
Always ensure your credentials and PIN remain confidential.
